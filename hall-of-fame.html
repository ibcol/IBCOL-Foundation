  • Results Intro

    The theme of this year’s IBCOL was to challenge teams to come up with a blockchain-based solution to one of the United Nation’s 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Between the 40 successful projects, an amazing 12 SDGs were tackled by the teams.

    Under “SDG Prize” column, the number refers to the SDG and the letter refers to the medal. So 2G means that team competed under the SDG “Zero Hunger” and got gold while 8HM means that team competed under “Decent Work and Economic Growth” and got an honourable mention.
